# PerX Document Authority

This document defines the hierarchy and authority of all strategic, policy, and technical documentation within the PerX ecosystem.

## Document Hierarchy

1. **Master Blueprint:** The highest-level strategic authority defining vision, mission, core values, and governance.
2. **Governance and policy documents:** Operational standards and constraints derived directly from the Master Blueprint.
3. **Product Strategy:** The definition of platform pillars, ecosystem flow, and MVP focus.
4. **Phased Roadmap:** The ordered progression of capability rollout and sector expansion.
5. **Ecosystem Architecture:** The alignment of strategic pillars with technical implementation status and modular design.
6. **MVP Architecture:** The technical backbone and application layer rules for the current production phase.
7. **Implementation Plan:** The prioritized list of technical tasks and guardrails.
8. **Feature specifications:** Detailed design and requirements for individual platform features.
9. **Code and tests:** The implementation reality.

## Alignment Rules

Lower-level documents must always align with and enforce the constraints established by higher-level documents. If a conflict arises, the higher-level document takes precedence.

**Important Note:** The presence of future regulated features (e.g., Live Escrow, Wallets, Real Estate) in the Master Blueprint or Phased Roadmap **does not** automatically activate or authorize their implementation in the codebase. All regulated features remain strictly subject to formal legal clearance, provider agreements, and compliance implementation before moving to lower levels of the document hierarchy.
