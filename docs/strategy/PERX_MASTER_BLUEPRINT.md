## 6. Executive Summary

PerX is a global trust-based commerce ecosystem. Its purpose is to improve how value is exchanged across industries, markets and borders through trusted, transparent, accountable and reliable systems.

While the platform initially focuses on opportunities such as freelance projects and jobs, it is a unified trust and commerce infrastructure intended to support diverse commercial sectors progressively.

## 7. Vision Statement

A global trust-based commerce ecosystem.

## 8. Mission Statement

Improve how value is exchanged across industries, markets and borders through trusted, transparent, accountable and reliable systems.

## 9. Trust

Trust surrounds every stage of the PerX ecosystem, rather than appearing only at payment. Trust architecture includes verifiable identities, transparent interactions, structured deal processes, simulated escrow states, and accountable reputation feedback loops.

Trust protection takes priority over short-term growth.

## 10. Transparency

Users should understand transaction states. Rules should be visible. Fees should be disclosed. Moderation decisions should be recorded. Trust scores should have understandable factors.

## 11. Accountability

Actions must be auditable. Users are responsible for commitments. Admin actions must be logged. Disputes require documented resolution. Platform mistakes must be corrected transparently.
