# PerX Product Strategy

## Strategic Pillars

The platform is structured around the following strategic pillars:

### Identity
Users, organizations and businesses establish trusted digital identities.
Includes:
* Profiles
* Verification
* Roles
* Capabilities
* History
* Reputation
* Trust signals

### Opportunity
People and organizations discover ways to exchange value.
Includes:
* Jobs
* Projects
* Services
* Partnerships
* Startups
* Co-founders
* Investments
* Property
* Trade
* Logistics
* Future sector opportunities

### Connection
Participants communicate and build commercial relationships.
Includes:
* Messaging
* Proposals
* Negotiation
* Collaboration
* Document sharing
* Professional networks

### Deal
Agreements are converted into structured commercial relationships.
Includes:
* Scope
* Terms
* Milestones
* Responsibilities
* Deliveries
* Approvals
* Records

### Transaction
Value is transferred through reliable and auditable systems.
Includes:
* Escrow-ready workflows
* Funding status
* Releases
* Refunds
* Ledger records
* Payment-provider integrations
* Multi-currency support

### Trust
The ecosystem reduces uncertainty and misconduct.
Includes:
* Verification
* Ratings
* Reviews
* Trust scores
* Fraud prevention
* Reports
* Disputes
* Moderation
* Audit trails

### Reputation
Successful activity strengthens future access to opportunity.
Includes:
* Transaction history
* Delivery performance
* Reliability
* Reviews
* Dispute history
* Verification
* Professional contribution

### Governance
Rules, enforcement and institutional accountability protect the ecosystem.
Includes:
* Policies
* Admin controls
* Moderation
* Appeals
* Audit logs
* Compliance
* Risk controls
* Sector-specific governance

## Ecosystem Flow
The foundational product flow is defined as:

Identity → Opportunity → Connection → Proposal → Agreement → Deal → Transaction → Delivery → Review → Reputation → New Opportunity

Trust surrounds every stage of this flow, rather than appearing only at the payment stage.

## Focused MVP
While the Master Blueprint is broad, the production MVP remains focused.
The current MVP prioritizes:
* Authentication
* Profiles
* Multiple ecosystem roles
* Jobs
* Freelance projects
* Discovery
* Messaging
* Proposals
* Deals
* Milestones
* Escrow-ready state management
* Deliveries
* Reviews
* Trust score
* Admin moderation

The MVP deliberately does **not** add full implementations for investment trading, real-estate transactions, logistics operations, international trade settlement, lending, banking, insurance, securities, cryptocurrency, or live escrow payments unless separately approved.
