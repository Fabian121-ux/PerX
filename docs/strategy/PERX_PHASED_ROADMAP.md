* Projects
* Search
* Messaging
* Proposals
* Deals

* Startup pages

* Startup roles
* Advisory opportunities

* Service marketplace
* Business-to-business opportunities

* Live payments
* Escrow provider integration
* Multi-currency transactions
* Refunds
* Dispute settlements
* Business wallets
* Institutional payment partnerships

## Phase 5 — Cross-Border Commerce Infrastructure
* International trade opportunities
* Logistics coordination
* Verified counterparties
* Cross-border transaction support
* Institutional integrations
* Trade documentation
* Regional compliance

## Phase 6 — Global Commerce Intelligence
* Ecosystem analytics
* Risk intelligence
* Fraud detection
* Reputation intelligence
* Market insights
* AI-assisted opportunity matching
* Institutional APIs

**Note:** Every future phase must depend on the stability of the previous foundation.
